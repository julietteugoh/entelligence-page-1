About entelligence

Empowering Beyond Borders: Our Transformative Journey

In the heart of Nigeria, our journey commences, driven by a vision to extend our impact far beyond borders. As we embark on this transformative odyssey, our mission is clear: to empower energy developers not only in Nigeria but across Africa and the expansive landscape of the global south. We are more than a company; we are architects of change, and our story is one that transcends geographical boundaries.

Flexible billing

Through our innovative solution, we enable energy developers to serve customers with and without bank accounts and debit cards, with or without access to stable internet, bridging the gap for all members of the community.

Remote monitoring

We offer hardware interface and robust software to collect generation and consumption data from remote distributed energy systems.

Intelligent operation

Our software offers data analytics capabilities, allowing offgrid energy developers to optimise their operations, reduce losses, and improve energy efficiency.

Tailored solution

We collaborate with offgrid and ongrid energy developers to tailor solutions to their specific needs.
